Ceremony
We will be having a civil ceremony in the heart of Copenhagen. We are only few guests attending, with all intentions being that the whole day be filled with familiar faces to create an intimate celebration.
The day will start with the ‘I Dos’ at City Hall, Rådhuset. As much as the setting is beautiful – it’s also no fuss, with the getting married part taking only 10 minutes. This will leave an opportunity for photos before we head off for the reception.
Reception
The location for the reception is still being confirmed. It will hopefully be close enough to travel on foot from City Hall.
Here we will have some booze to celebrate.
A chance for us to breathe, laugh, kiss, hug and enjoy a drink (or three), have a light bite to eat and make the most of the setting to take some photos.
Tucker
Straight from Copenhagen’s most local farm, ØsterGro’s rooftop restaurant will be taking care of the food and (many) drinks for the evening!
The food concept is founded on good organic produce delivered from local growers, producers and hunters. The menu changes with the season and will be accompanied by a good selection of natural wines, beers and non-alcoholic drinks to suit everyone’s taste.
The food is served family style, a truly social dining experience
Cocktails
Rarely have we met a cocktail we haven’t enjoyed.
After dinner we will head into town for a drink, a cocktail, a nightcap and a good dance. It may be Brønnum, TaTa, Curfew or Ruby’s...
Accomodation
For those of you who are travelling, a nice place, with a late check-out time would be ideal.
Most of the activities will be taking place in the close-ish proximity to the centre of town.
Here are some recommendations: Nobis, SP34 or Skt. Annæ
(naturally there are plenty more, just ask)
Transit
We will make sure transport is sorted for the activities of the day.
There will be a natural break between the reception and dinner for everyone to catch a breath and ready-up for dinner and drinks.
Guests will have to organise travel here, depending on how they (you) choose to spend the hours in between.
